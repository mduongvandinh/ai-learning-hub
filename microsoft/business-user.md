## Tips & Best Practices

### Prompting Tips

1. **Be specific**: Thay vì "write email", dùng "write a professional email declining a meeting invitation, keeping positive relationship"

2. **Provide context**: "As the marketing manager, reviewing Q4 campaign results..."

3. **Specify format**: "In bullet points", "as a table", "in 3 paragraphs"

4. **Iterate**: Refine responses với follow-up prompts

### Security Awareness

- [ ] Hiểu data trong Copilot
- [ ] Sensitivity labels
- [ ] Không share confidential info
- [ ] Review AI outputs trước khi send
