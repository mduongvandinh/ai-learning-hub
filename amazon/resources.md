## Cost Management

### Free Tier Tips

```
Maximize free tier:
1. SageMaker: 250 hours/month (first 2 months)
2. Lambda: 1M requests/month
3. Bedrock: Limited trial
4. Q Developer: Free tier
```

### Cost Optimization

- Use PartyRock (free) for learning
- SageMaker Studio Lab (free)
- Spot instances for training
- Reserved capacity for production
